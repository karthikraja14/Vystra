Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

At Vystra ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through vystra.in, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to give us, such as when you submit our contact form:

  • Your name and email address
  • The project type and message details you provide
  • Basic, anonymous usage data (e.g. pages visited) if analytics is enabled

2. How We Use It

  • To respond to your enquiry and discuss your project
  • To provide, quote and deliver our services
  • To improve our website and offering

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

3. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, which delivers your message to us by email. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policy. We may also use privacy- friendly analytics to understand site traffic.

4. Data Retention

We keep enquiry information only as long as needed to serve you and for reasonable business records. You may ask us to delete your data at any time.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us at admin@vystra.in.

6. Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies, primarily for essential functionality and optional analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at admin@vystra.in or on WhatsApp.
