Every plan includes free support (1-6 months). Minor changes on us. After that, maintenance plans start at ₹2,999/mo.

Every build includes SEO fundamentals — meta tags, clean URLs, structured data, fast loading, mobile optimization. Advanced SEO strategy available as add-on.

Yes. We audit your current site, identify gaps, build a modern replacement. Content migration and SEO preservation included.

Transparent, fixed pricing per plan. No hourly rates, no surprises. Custom quotes for complex projects after a free discovery call.
